Voice & Language Guide

How Grow Your Practice sounds. Reference this for all copy.

Tone

Warm, knowledgeable, never condescending. Speak to therapists as smart professionals who just haven't had time to learn this yet. Emphasize safety, ethics, and client care alongside business growth. No hype, no hustle culture.

✓ Say this
  • Practice (not "business")
  • Clients (not "customers")
  • Grow (not "scale")
  • Streamline (not "optimize")
  • Income streams (not "monetize")
  • Practical (not "disruptive")
✗ Never say
  • Hustle
  • Grind
  • Scale
  • Hack / life hack
  • Disrupt
  • Leverage (as a verb)

Brand Story

It's not a rocket. It's not a chart going up and to the right. It's a living thing, growing at its own pace. That's what your practice is — and that's how we think about AI. Not as a disruption, but as a tool that grows with you.
